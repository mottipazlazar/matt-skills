# Buyer Avatar Categories & Research Framework

Source: Dealflow Buyer Avatars Sales Team Expanded

---

## How Avatar Research Works

For each new listing, identify the most likely buyer type based on:
- County and state (rural vs suburban vs desert)
- Price range (budget buyer vs investor vs builder)
- Lot size (small homesite vs large acreage)
- Zoning and land use rules
- Proximity to towns, lakes, attractions
- Deal type (cash vs owner financing)

Research the area online to understand:
- Who is buying land there right now?
- What are the emotional drivers?
- What are the fears and objections?
- What lifestyle does this area enable?

Present up to 3 avatar categories with reasoning. Let the user choose which to focus on.
The chosen avatar drives all copy decisions.

**IMPORTANT:** These avatars are distinct categories. Do not combine or confuse them.
In particular:
- **Recreational** (#5) = camping, hunting, fishing, ATV, outdoor fun ON the land
- **Land Banking** (#10) = investment hold for appreciation, not physical use
- **Future Builder** (#3) = plans to build a home eventually, uses the land as a future homesite
These are three separate buyer types with different fears, desires, and messaging.

## Avatar 1: Middle-Class Family-Focused Legacy Seeker ("John")
- **Age:** 40s-50s
- **Desire:** Leave a legacy, own peaceful land, self-sufficiency, space for family, long-term security.
- **Fears:** Financial regret, choosing the wrong county/zoning, isolation, unexpected fees or restrictions.
- **Where to Find Them:** Facebook groups, YouTube (DIY/off-grid), family finance content, weekend researchers.
- **Messaging:** "Lock in a place your family can grow into. Buy now, build later, and avoid the regret of buying too small."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Are you planning to build soon or holding for the future?" "Do you need utilities nearby or off-grid is fine?"

## Avatar 2: Retiree Relocator & Fixed-Income Planner
- **Age:** 55+
- **Desire:** Quiet, stability, predictable ownership, future homesite, low-stress decision, community feel (not remote).
- **Fears:** Complexity, being too far from services, zoning surprises, buying land that is not realistically buildable.
- **Where to Find Them:** Facebook/AARP-style groups, retirement forums, YouTube (downsizing), local community pages.
- **Messaging:** "A calm homesite in the right context. Secure land now and build at your pace."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you want city-limits/utilities context?" "How soon are you thinking of building?"

## Avatar 3: Future Builder (Buy Now, Build Later) - Utility-Dependent
- **Age:** 30-60
- **Desire:** A buildable residential lot with clear rules, ability to plan without rushing, utilities path that makes sense.
- **Fears:** Zoning that sounds residential but is not practically buildable, missing water/sewer, permit dead-ends.
- **Where to Find Them:** Zillow/Redfin browsers, new-construction forums, Facebook local groups, "moving to..." communities.
- **Messaging:** "Plan it right: clear zoning, clear thresholds, and a lot you can verify before you commit to construction."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you need both water and sewer?" "Do you want buildable-by-right lots only?"

## Avatar 4: Off-Grid / Homestead Freedom Seeker
- **Age:** 30-65
- **Desire:** Independence, privacy, space, minimal rules, solar/well/septic (where allowed), workshop/garden/animals (where allowed).
- **Fears:** Hidden covenants, enforcement, access issues, being misled about what is allowed (RV, mobile, camping).
- **Where to Find Them:** Off-grid YouTube, homesteading FB groups, prepper communities, RV/off-road channels.
- **Messaging:** "Own land you control. Use it now, improve it later, and keep your options open."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you want RV/camping use?" "Do you care about proximity to a town?"

## Avatar 5: Recreational Buyers (Camping, Hunting, Fishing, ATV)
- **Age:** 25-65
- **Desire:** Weekend escape, privacy, outdoor access, simple entry, a basecamp near water/trails/public land.
- **Fears:** No legal access, restrictions on camping/RV, neighbors too close, land that is unusable seasonally.
- **Where to Find Them:** Outdoors forums, hunting/fishing FB groups, RV and ATV groups, YouTube channels.
- **Messaging:** "A private basecamp you can actually use. Clear access, clear rules, simple ownership."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Is your main goal camping/RV or holding long-term?" "How important is road quality?"

## Avatar 6: Seasonal RV / Snowbird Basecamp Buyer
- **Age:** 35-70
- **Desire:** A legal place to park seasonally, store gear, and return every year without paying nightly fees.
- **Fears:** Time limits, permit requirements, neighbors reporting them, access getting muddy or blocked.
- **Where to Find Them:** RV forums, snowbird communities, Facebook RV travel groups.
- **Messaging:** "A repeatable basecamp - your own place to return to, without depending on rentals."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "How many months per year do you plan to stay?" "Do you need RV allowances confirmed?"

## Avatar 7: Alternative Living (Manufactured, Tiny, Container - Where Allowed)
- **Age:** 25-50
- **Desire:** Creative housing, lower cost of living, speed to setup, flexibility in structure type.
- **Fears:** HOA interference, zoning bans, minimum size requirements, city limits restrictions.
- **Where to Find Them:** Tiny home YouTube, Reddit, minimalist groups, alternative housing FB groups.
- **Messaging:** "Build your lifestyle your way - as long as the county rules support it. We will keep it honest."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Are you on wheels or a permanent foundation?" "Do you need manufactured allowed by-right?"

## Avatar 8: Small Builders & Infill Developers
- **Age:** 30-65
- **Desire:** Shovel-ready or near-ready lots, utilities nearby, predictable approvals, resale comps that support new builds.
- **Fears:** Utility gaps, floodplain issues, odd setbacks/lot width problems, slow approvals, unclear title.
- **Where to Find Them:** Local builder networks, contractor groups, county meetings, investor forums.
- **Messaging:** "Clean lot, clear constraints, quick DD pack. If it fits your model, we move fast."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "What is your minimum lot size/width?" "Do you require water/sewer at the curb?"

## Avatar 9: Investors & Land Traders (Flippers/Wholesalers)
- **Age:** 25-60
- **Desire:** Buy low, resell fast, clear comps, simple story, optional exits (cash resale or terms resale).
- **Fears:** Overpaying, illiquidity, messy restrictions, access/title issues that kill the flip.
- **Where to Find Them:** Facebook investor groups, Landmodo/Land.com, Craigslist, BiggerPockets-style forums.
- **Messaging:** "Clean land deals with clear exit paths. Simple story, easy to position."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you want cash-only inventory or terms-friendly?" "What margin do you need?"

## Avatar 10: Land Banking / Inflation Hedge Buyer
- **Age:** 30-70
- **Desire:** Hold land long-term, low carrying costs, future optionality, "they are not making more land."
- **Fears:** Buying in the wrong area, future restrictions, parcels with no realistic demand.
- **Where to Find Them:** Finance channels, real estate newsletters, conservative investing communities.
- **Messaging:** "A simple land hold you can keep for years. Low drama, high optionality."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Are you holding 5-10+ years?" "Do you want rural or near-growth corridors?"

## Avatar 11: Multi-Generation / Adjacent Lots / Compound Planner
- **Age:** 35-70
- **Desire:** Keep family close but independent, future builds without conflict, space buffers, expansion options.
- **Fears:** Crowding, regret buying too small, needing to move later, neighbors building too close.
- **Where to Find Them:** Family relocation groups, homestead groups, retirement + family planners, builder referrals.
- **Messaging:** "Buy once, plan later. Adjacent lots give you space, separation, and long-term control."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you want 2-4 lots side-by-side?" "Planning multiple dwellings?"

## Avatar 12: Cash-Only Bargain Hunter (Auction Mindset)
- **Age:** 25-65
- **Desire:** Lowest price, quick close, instant ownership, "steal" deals.
- **Fears:** Paying retail, hidden costs, being upsold, slow closing.
- **Where to Find Them:** Craigslist, auction watchers, deal forums, Facebook Marketplace.
- **Messaging:** "Clean, direct cash deal. No fluff - just the facts and a fast close."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Are you paying cash today?" "Do you want a discount for fast close?"

## Avatar 13: Terms-First Monthly-Budget Buyer (Credit-Challenged Friendly)
- **Age:** 25-55
- **Desire:** Ownership without a bank, low entry, predictable monthly payment, path to something they own.
- **Fears:** Big down payments, credit checks, hidden fees, losing money if plans change.
- **Where to Find Them:** Facebook Marketplace, rent-to-own communities, alternative finance groups.
- **Messaging:** "No bank needed. Low entry. Simple payments. Clear rules."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "What is comfortable monthly?" "Do you need to use the land now or later?"

## Avatar 14: Utilities-Ready Neighborhood Buyer (City Limits / Suburban Context)
- **Age:** 30-70
- **Desire:** Established neighborhood, paved/gravel access, utilities in the area, residential context with nearby homes.
- **Fears:** Remote parcels, lack of services, unpredictable approvals, weird restrictions.
- **Where to Find Them:** Local Facebook groups, Zillow browsing, relocation groups.
- **Messaging:** "Real residential context - not raw desert. The kind of lot that fits long-term housing plans."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Do you need city limits?" "Do you want a neighborhood with existing homes nearby?"

## Avatar 15: Water-Adjacent Lifestyle Buyer (Creek/Lake/River Proximity)
- **Age:** 25-70
- **Desire:** Proximity to water for lifestyle, views, weekend use, or future homesite near parks/lakes.
- **Fears:** Floodplain surprises, access issues, seasonal limitations, restrictions near waterways.
- **Where to Find Them:** Lake community groups, fishing groups, local recreation forums.
- **Messaging:** "Water proximity without the tourist pricing. Clear layout and transparent flood information."
- **Qualifying Questions:** "Is water proximity your main driver?" "Are you okay with floodplain considerations?"

## Avatar Selection Process

1. Review property details and area research
2. Match property characteristics to avatar desires and fears
3. Consider price point and deal type (owner financing attracts different avatars than cash-only)
4. Present top 1-3 matches with reasoning
5. User selects the primary avatar or blend
6. All subsequent copy targets that avatar's fears, dreams, and language

---

## Using the Avatar in Copy

Once selected, the avatar influences:
- **Hook:** What stops them scrolling (their specific fear or dream)
- **Body:** What their internal dialogue sounds like
- **Features emphasis:** Which property details matter most to them
- **Risk reversal:** What specific fears to address
- **CTA:** What action feels natural for their situation
- **Urgency:** What makes "now" compelling for their goal
- **Platform choice:** Where this avatar is most likely browsing
- **Title direction:** Which creative category (Curiosity, Bold, Visual, etc.) best reaches them
